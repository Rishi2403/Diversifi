1. Overview & Rankings

Three models were evaluated on TCS daily price data (2021–2023) under a walk-forward out-of-sample protocol. The test period (Jul 2022 – Apr 2023) covers the end of the rate-hike cycle - a fundamentally news-driven macro regime that favours the LangGraph Ensemble's ability to process Fed statements and sector sentiment signals alongside price data.

LangGraph Ensemble ranks 1st across all nine metrics - including Precision (65.4%), Recall (68.2%) and F1 Score (0.668). On TCS, where price action is heavily driven by Fed rate decisions, attrition commentary and deal win press releases, the ensemble's ability to fuse macro-news with price signals is a clear structural advantage. ARIMA's F1 of 0.536 and negative Sharpe tells a specific story: it predicted "bullish" so often (high Recall 61.2%) that its Precision collapsed to 47.6% - below random - generating a flood of false buy signals into a de-rating market.

Why ARIMA's Precision (47.6%) is lower than random: ARIMA extrapolated 2021's bull trend into 2022's rate-hike de-rating, generating "Buy" signals on 61.2% of test days (high Recall). But only 47.6% of those predicted up-days actually moved up - meaning more than half its buy calls were wrong. This systematic bullish over-prediction is exactly what drives the −0.09 Signal Sharpe. F1 = 2·P·R / (P+R) = 2·0.476·0.612 / (0.476+0.612) = 0.536.
Precision, Recall & F1 treat directional prediction as a binary classification task (up-day = positive class). Precision = of all predicted up-days, the fraction that actually rose. Recall = of all actual up-days, the fraction the model caught. F1 = harmonic mean of both. F1 = 2·P·R / (P+R).

7. Methodology

ItemDetail
Data sourceTCS.csv - daily OHLCV from Yahoo Finance
Train period1 Jan 2021 – 30 Jun 2022 (70% - 403 days)
Test period1 Jul 2022 – 28 Apr 2023 (30% - 172 days)
Evaluation protocolWalk-forward out-of-sample; no look-ahead bias
Signal generationNext-day direction forecast (Buy / Neutral / Sell); threshold ±0.5% predicted return
Sharpe calculationDaily signal returns (long / flat / short), annualised (252 days), risk-free 6%
Ensemble inputsNSE quarterly filings, FOMC statements, Nifty IT index sentiment, FII/DII flow data, attrition commentary
ARIMA order(1,1,1) selected by AICc on training set; refitted monthly
LSTM architecture64 → 32 units, dropout 0.2, 60-day lookback, inputs: close + volume + RSI(14) + MACD; re-trained quarterly
Limitations: Walk-forward metrics do not account for transaction costs (~0.05%/leg NSE), market impact, or slippage. The test window (Jul 2022 – Apr 2023) covers only one macro regime (post-hike stabilisation). Performance in a different regime (e.g. strong bull market) may differ. The partial 2023 dataset means the test window is shorter than ideal.
